Project Proposal

Project name:

Nourish

Overview:

I want to work on the redesign of the Nourish website. I think that getting more research on the users will make this website more user friendly. In addition to redesigning the website, I think making it responsive for mobile devices is important because many people use their phones more often than a laptop or desktop. I also want to add photos that I take myself to add to the website instead of using free stock photos. I think that adding photos that are more accurate to the targeted audience makes the website more authentic. I want to make the site very consistent with design as well. I think that color is very important when it comes to food related things.

Audience:

Anyone who relies on the Blue Ridge area Food Bank and has access to the internet.

Timeline:

  • User research - due 2/20
  • Prototype and wireframes - due 2/23
